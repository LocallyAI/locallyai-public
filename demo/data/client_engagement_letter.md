## 4. Anti-Money-Laundering Checks

Under the Money Laundering, Terrorist Financing and Transfer of Funds (Information on the Payer) Regulations 2017, we must verify your identity and the source of any funds before substantively acting. Please return the attached AML form together with certified copies of:

- a current passport or photo driving licence;
- a utility bill or bank statement dated within the last three months.

For corporate clients, we will additionally require Companies House documentation and beneficial-owner identification for all owners with a 25% or greater interest.

We may not be able to start work or release funds until these checks are complete.

## 5. Confidentiality and Data Protection

All information you provide will be treated as confidential and used solely for the purpose of this matter. We process personal data in accordance with the UK GDPR and our privacy notice (available at [URL]). You can find more about how we handle data, including your data-subject rights, in our Data Protection Policy.

## 6. Limitation of Liability

Our total liability to you in respect of this matter is limited to **£[•] million**, which corresponds to the level of professional indemnity insurance we maintain. This cap does not apply to liability for fraud, dishonesty, death, or personal injury caused by negligence, or anything else that cannot lawfully be limited.

## 7. Complaints

If you are unhappy with any aspect of our service, please raise it first with the Supervising Partner. If we cannot resolve it together, you may escalate the complaint to our Complaints Partner, [Name], who will respond within 14 days. You also have the right to complain to the Legal Ombudsman within six months of our final response, and ultimately to the Solicitors Regulation Authority on conduct matters.

## 8. Termination

You may terminate our retainer at any time. We may terminate only on reasonable notice and for good reason (for example, conflict of interest, non-payment of invoices, or loss of trust and confidence). On termination, we will issue a final bill for work done and disbursements incurred to that date.
